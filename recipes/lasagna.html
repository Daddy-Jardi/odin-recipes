World's Best Lasagna

Description

This is the perfect recipe for a delicious lasagna. You will be able to custimize it to your preference.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato Product
  • Sugar
  • Seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let rest
